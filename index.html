01 · Project overview
Objective

Build a scalable campaign system

Design repeatable marketing workflows that reduce planning time by 40% and improve cross-team visibility on all active campaigns.

Target audience

Retail Traders & Investors

Active day traders, F&O participants, and retail investors seeking real-time market data and AI-driven stock insights. Fast-paced, data-hungry, and mobile-first.

Business goals

Pipeline & brand growth

Generate 200+ MQLs per quarter, grow organic reach by 3×, and establish TickerKey as a trusted fintech brand in retail investor circles.

Key challenges

Fragmented tools & data silos

Disconnected CRM data, inconsistent messaging across channels, and no single source of truth for campaign performance.
